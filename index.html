How to play “Simple CS”: Rules of CSGO

  1. Be prepared for anything, (random pushes through smokes, random peeks) and play accordingly hold angles with focus even if you are sure no one will peek.
  2. Don’t have “lazy” crosshair placement make sure your crosshair is always honed on the next most likely position for the enemy to peek even when rotating only remove your crosshair from the position when you are sure you are safe.
  3. Don’t reload your weapon unnecessarily, unless you are completely safe from the enemy and know they won’t hear you as the act of reloading can cause some enemies to play agro trying to catch you off-guard.
  4. Don’t pull out your knife or grenade if there is any chance of the enemy peeking you at all, guns will always do more damage than grenades more consistently and don’t leave you vulnerable for ~ 3 seconds.
  5. Cover positions your teammates are not, always be looking for gaps in your defences or possible flanks from the enemies.
  6. Always buy as a team, don’t go against the buy of the team, and buy hero guns consistently as this will put your team at a huge disadvantage when they can buy and you can’t.
  7. Don’t give away your side's advantage for free (Don’t give away your position as a CT, like running or making noise or throwing random utility / Don’t downgrade from an AK to a M4 during a round and try not to peek predictably)
  8. Peek angles individually while practising proper pre-aiming and crosshair placement and don’t overexpose yourself to multiple angles, unless you are rushing in this case you have to move fast and there is a purpose behind your peek.
  9. In any situation when you might encounter an enemy, try to make sure you have a teammate to trade you as this will immediately improve your odds of winning any fight you take, as you usually peek more confidently as you are not afraid of dying and confidence is crucial in gunfights and even if you die, your teammate should be able to kill the person who killed you.
  10. Learn how to trade and do it constantly, trading is one of the most important skills you can learn and master as it will significantly improve the chances of you winning games and getting impactful kills.
  11. After every kill change positions, this is for both CT and T side as this allows you to keep the positional advantage and constantly catch the enemy off-guard which increases your chances of winning the next fight.
  12. Use sound to your advantage, listen to everything from: where gunshots are coming from (learn to recognise decoys just by their sound) / Listen for footsteps and understand how far away from you people can hear your footsteps (Radar zoom 0.85 gives you an idea of distance you can be hear while running or can hear enemies)
  13. Use a mixture of preemptive and reactive utility throughout the game (Before seeing the enemy and after seeing the enemy, if you favor 1 too heavily the enemy can notice this and take advantage by either rushing if you are reactive or slowing down and waiting if you are preemptive)
  14. Learn to Study your opponents and try doing it subconsciously look for things like (Weaker players / Weaponry choices (Awp? Shotgun?) / Economic decisions (Are they saving often? do they have a lot of weak buys?) / Positioning decisions (Most players sit in the same 3-5 spots on each site) / Play decisions (Agro / passive) / Utility choices(preemptive/reactive))
  15. Do not be hungry for kills this can lead to you overextending and dying early or potentially throwing away rounds you should win, also although getting an ace is nice focus on winning the game over looking good, this will put you in good stead for your career and do not harbour any resentment for anyone that you think has “stolen” your ace or clip.
  16. Use timing && movement to your advantage whenever possible, this will allow you to get into positions the enemy might think are not possible and give you the advantage in any fight you take, although pulling out a knife to make a jump during a gunfight is not advisable, preferably do any movements or positional changes significantly before enemies are spotted and before they can hear you.
  17. Once in a position do not change or throw away your position advantage for a marginally better position instead focus on the gunfights that are about to endure, by simply focusing on the fights this will put you at a larger advantage than the positional change and losing the element of surprise.
  18. Whenever possible play as a team, “Ape together strong”, now doing individual plays are acceptable but should never be the norm otherwise they become predictable and easy to counter and then just put your team at a disadvantage.
  19. Do not bait in an attempt to make yourself look good or get a highlight, you should be able to use and help your teammates at the same time, if you are able to get 2 kills and keep your teammate alive, that is significantly better than getting 3 kills and letting your teammate die.
  20. Never blame your teammates, if the only person you can blame is yourself then you look a lot more deeply into your game rather than passing the blame onto someone else and refusing to accept that you had some part to play in the loss, never make excuses like “I did my job” or “I dropped 28 kills so its not my fault” because in the grand scheme of the game these things do not matter and when you look deeply into your own game, you improve significantly quicker, than someone who does not accept blame.
  21. Toxicity kills teamwork/communication and coordination, which is one of the quickest ways to lose a match and shut down mentally, never start toxicity as it is unnecessary in all situations (Remember you are playing a game there is no need to be nasty), and if someone else is being toxic judge their ability to communicate and coordinate with the team although there will be outbursts does the information he provides and the ability to guide him out-weigh his toxicity? if not mute him but still drop to him whenever possible and give callouts just because you have him muted doesn’t mean he has you muted.
  22. When communicating make sure to state the following info as concisely and as quickly as possible: Number / Tagged / Position / Weapon for example “One tagged 60 back platform B, with awp” | “Two pushing long house with pistols” | “One dead short had AK” | “Three Rushing long, one tagged 60, they had AKs” If no damage is done you can drop the tagged portion and if dead no real need to state weapon Note if info is older than 30 seconds disregard it except weapon type and damage dealt as this could be useful if asked for.
